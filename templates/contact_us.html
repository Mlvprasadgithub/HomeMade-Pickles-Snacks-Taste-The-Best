Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the pickles last?

Our pickles have a shelf life of 6-12 months when stored properly in a cool, dry place. Each product has an expiry date mentioned on the packaging.

Do you use any preservatives?

No, we use only natural ingredients and traditional preservation methods. Our pickles are free from artificial preservatives and chemicals.

What is your delivery time?

We typically deliver within 3-5 business days for most locations. Express delivery options are also available for faster shipping.

Do you offer bulk orders?

Yes, we offer wholesale pricing for bulk orders. Please contact us directly for custom pricing and minimum order quantities.
